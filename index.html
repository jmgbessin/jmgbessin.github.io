Introduction

This website that I made has the goal of providing information and inspiration, not for scientists but for normal, curious people. I am a high school kid who recently got interested in the world of physics and chemistry. This website, though, focuses more on the quantum aspect of these subjects (the microscopic world), but there may be a few deviations. I don't present my information in any particular order, but fill it up as I learn.

I really incourage people to get more knowledge about this fascinating world, and getting a strong foundation to build your knowledge upon is important. I usually cringe when I hear about information people present that will "change your view of the world", but physics has done just that. I don't see simple things like force, gravity, light, time and space, the same as I would before.

Learning about physics is also a great way to impress your parents or show off. It also might seem meaningless to get ahead of a school program if you are in High School, but it actually helps you understand better what you are learning and how to interpret it. Have fun reading, my information is written in the subjects section.

Bear in mind, if you are coming for simple introductions, that is not what I present. Without having expert talk and discoveries, I do mostly skip the basics and head right to the subject I am talking about. These are mostly miscellaneous subjects based on my insights and questions that I have asked myself.
